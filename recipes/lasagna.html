Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and sauce. It's a hearty and comforting meal that's perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

    Ingredients:
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    Instructions:
  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess fat.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper.
  5. Spread a layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, ground beef, marinara sauce, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers.
  7. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  8. Bake for 30-40 minutes until bubbly and golden brown.
