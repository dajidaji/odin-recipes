Tomato Salad Recipe

This is a refreshing recipe for a simple tomato salad, perfect for summer.

    Ingredients
  • 4 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. In a large bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and basil.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  4. Serve immediately or refrigerate for 30 minutes to let the flavors meld.
  5. Enjoy your fresh tomato salad!
